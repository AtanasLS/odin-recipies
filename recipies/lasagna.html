How to make a Lasagna

Ingridients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 cooked lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook and stir the ground beef until browned. Drain off excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until the onion is tender.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  4. Season with sugar, salt, basil, fennel seeds, and parsley.
  5. Simmer the sauce, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the eggs.
  7. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. To assemble the lasagna, spread a portion of the meat sauce evenly in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  9. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the meat sauce.
  10. Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  11. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  12. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  14. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  16. Let it stand for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
