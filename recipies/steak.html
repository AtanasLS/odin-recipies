Classic Steak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions:

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature, about 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat your grill or grill pan to high heat.
  3. Rub the steaks with minced garlic and olive oil. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle fresh rosemary and thyme leaves over the steaks and press them gently into the meat.
  5. Place the steaks on the hot grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the time to your desired level of doneness.
  6. During the last minute of cooking, add a tablespoon of butter to the top of each steak and let it melt and coat the meat.
  7. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Slice and serve the delicious grilled steaks!
